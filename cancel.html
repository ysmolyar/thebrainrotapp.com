Manage Your Subscription

Not sure how you subscribed? If you were charged by Apple (check your email for a receipt from Apple), follow the App Store instructions. If you were charged directly by credit card, follow the Direct Subscription instructions.

App Store Subscriptions (via Apple)

Switch Plans via Apple

Want to switch from weekly to monthly or annual? You can change your plan anytime:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Tap Brainrot
  5. Choose a different plan under Subscription Options

Your new plan will start at the end of your current billing period.

Cancel via Apple

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Tap Brainrot
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Request a Refund from Apple

Refunds for App Store subscriptions are handled directly by Apple:

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your Brainrot purchase
  4. Select Request a refund

Direct Subscriptions (via Credit Card)

Switch Plans, Cancel, or Request a Refund

If you subscribed directly with a credit card (not through the App Store), you can manage everything on our subscription management page.

Enter the email you used to subscribe, and you'll be able to:

  • View your subscription details
  • Switch to a different plan
  • Cancel your subscription
  • Request a refund for your most recent charge
