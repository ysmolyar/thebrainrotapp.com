brainrot FAQ

Everything you need to know about reclaiming your day

What is brainrot? +

brainrot is a screen time control app that helps you reduce phone usage through a unique visual approach. It displays a brain avatar that deteriorates with excessive app usage, gamifying the process of reducing screen time. Users save an average of 1 hour 4 minutes daily.

How much does brainrot cost? +

brainrot is free to download with optional in-app purchases. Subscription options include: Monthly subscription for $3.99, and yearly subscriptions ranging from $9.99 to $49.99 depending on the plan you choose.

How does brainrot protect my privacy? +

brainrot uses Apple's Screen Time API for monitoring and blocking. All processing runs entirely on your device - no browsing data ever leaves your phone. We take privacy seriously and don't collect or store your personal usage data.

I'm having issues blocking/unblocking apps +

First, try the refresh button at the top of the help page - this resolves 99% of blocking issues.

If that doesn't work:

  1. Turn it off and on again
    • Disable and re-enable rules in the rules tab
    • Close and reopen brainrot
    • Restart your device if needed
  2. Remove iOS limits or downtimes
    • Remove any Screen Time limits from iOS or other apps
    • Then try step 1 again
  3. Force quit background apps
    • Memory limitations can cause issues
    • Quitting background apps helps clear memory

Why is my estimated screen time inaccurate? +

Screen time data relies on Apple's Screen Time system, which can sometimes have delays. Certain calculations, like brain health, rely on estimated screen time which can deviate from actual screen time. We strive for high accuracy but cannot guarantee 100% accuracy due to system limitations.

Why is my health score so low? +

Your health score is calculated based on your screen time compared to your set goal. Exceeding your daily goal lowers the score. Check the main view for a detailed breakdown of your usage and how it affects your brain health score.

How do I customize my screen time goal? +

You can adjust your daily screen time goal in the app's settings section. This allows you to set realistic targets based on your lifestyle and gradually reduce your screen time.

What devices and iOS versions does brainrot support? +

brainrot requires iOS 17.4 or later and is compatible with iPhone and iPad. It's optimized for all iPhone models and works seamlessly with Apple's Screen Time API.

Is brainrot available for Android? +

Currently, brainrot is only available for iOS devices. We're exploring Android development - join our waitlist to be notified when an Android version becomes available.

Which apps and websites can brainrot block? +

brainrot can block any app or website on your device. You can create custom schedules and rules for different apps, set daily time allowances, and even block entire categories of apps or specific websites.

How do I report a bug or request a feature? +

To report bugs or suggest features, visit https://brainrot.featurebase.app/ or email us at ysmolyar@gmail.com. We actively review all feedback to improve the app.

Why are there data loading issues in the app? +

Data loading depends on Apple's Screen Time system. If you experience loading issues, try refreshing the page one or more times. This usually resolves any temporary data sync problems.

Can I use brainrot on multiple devices? +

Yes, if you have a subscription, you can use brainrot on multiple iOS devices signed in with the same Apple ID. Your settings and rules will sync across devices.

How do I cancel my subscription? +

You can cancel your subscription via your device Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions > brainrot > Cancel Subscription. The subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

What makes brainrot different from other screen time apps? +

brainrot uniquely gamifies screen time reduction with a visual brain avatar that deteriorates with excessive usage. This humorous yet effective approach helps 94% of our members feel less distracted, with users saving over an hour daily on average.
