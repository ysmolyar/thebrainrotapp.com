Cancel Your Subscription

There are two ways to subscribe to Brainrot — through the App Store (Apple) or directly with a credit card. How you cancel depends on which method you used.

How to Tell Which One You Have

  • Direct subscription (credit card): Check your email for a message from Brainrot (support+brainrot@superwall.app). If you have one, you subscribed directly — follow the Direct Subscription instructions below.
  • App Store subscription (via Apple): On your iPhone, go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions. If Brainrot is listed there, you subscribed through Apple — follow the App Store instructions below.

Cancel an App Store Subscription (via Apple)

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Tap Brainrot
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again.

Cancel a Direct Subscription (via Credit Card)

If you subscribed directly with a credit card (not through the App Store), you can cancel on our subscription management page.

  1. Go to brainrot.superwall.app/manage
  2. Enter the email you used to subscribe
  3. Follow the instructions to cancel your subscription

Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again.
