Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Information Collection

Required permissions:

  • Screen Time: to monitor and analyze your device usage patterns
  • Notifications: to send you mindfulness reminders and alerts

2. Data Usage and Protection

Your screen time information is:

  • Analyzed to provide usage insights
  • Used to generate usage reports and trends
  • Stored locally on your device
  • Not shared with third parties
  • Not transmitted to our servers

3. Data Collection and Usage

3.1 Data Not Linked to Your Identity

The following data may be collected but is not linked to your identity:

  • Usage data
  • Product interaction: how you interact with app features and functionality
  • Anonymous analytics data for app improvement

Product interaction data is collected and used solely for:

  • App functionality (authenticating users, enabling features, preventing fraud, implementing security measures)
  • Ensuring server up-time and app performance
  • Minimizing app crashes
  • Improving scalability and performance
  • Providing customer support
  • Understanding how features are used to improve the App

3.2 Payment and Subscription Data

When you subscribe to Brainrot, the following data may be collected depending on your subscription method:

App Store subscriptions (via Apple):

  • Payment and subscription management is handled entirely by Apple
  • We do not receive or store your payment details
  • See Apple's Privacy Policy for details

Direct subscriptions (via credit card):

  • Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card information
  • Your email address is collected for subscription management and is stored securely
  • Subscription management events (lookups, cancellations, refunds) are logged for fraud prevention and support purposes
  • See Stripe's Privacy Policy for details on payment data handling

3.3 Third-Party Services

Brainrot uses the following third-party services:

Mixpanel:

  • Collects anonymous usage data to understand how features are used
  • Helps identify and fix bugs or performance issues
  • Data is aggregated and anonymized
  • Used solely for app improvement purposes

Firebase:

  • Provides authentication, crash reporting, and performance monitoring
  • Collects anonymous device and usage data
  • Used for app stability and improvement

Meta Pixel and TikTok Pixel (website only):

  • Used on certain pages of our website to measure advertising effectiveness
  • May collect browsing data such as page views
  • Not used within the App itself
  • You can opt out via your browser's privacy settings or ad preferences

All third-party data collection is:

  • Used only for app functionality, improvements, or advertising measurement
  • Not sold or shared with other parties beyond the stated purposes

Your core screen time data remains:

  • Locally on your device
  • Without external data transmission
  • Completely private and secure

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the App
  • Control app permissions through device settings
  • Request deletion of any server-side data associated with your email by contacting us

5. Data Retention

  • Local app data remains until you uninstall the App
  • Subscription management data is retained for fraud prevention and support purposes

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this privacy policy through the App or via email.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about privacy or data protection, please contact us at thebrainrotapp@gmail.com
